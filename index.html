Project-2
Sarcasm Detection In Telugu Language

The Project Aim is to detect whether the sentence are Sarcastic or Non-sarcastic the telugu data which is collected from the popular tv shows is collected and annotated as sarcastic and non-sarcastic which are 1800(1300 sarcastic, 500 non-sarcastic) done preprocessing steps like Tokenization, Removal of punctuations,Removal of Stop Words, Generate POS-Tags and Used TF-IDF vectorizer to vectorize the sentences (using Just sentences, Sentences with POS-tags , trigram) to train machine learning models. Word Embeddings are used to train Deep Learning models like RNN, LSTM and GRU and achieved and accuracy of 90%
